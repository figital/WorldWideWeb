Operation

  • Document is displayed one page at a time (default is 23 lines).
  • A prompt is produced at the bottom of each page.
  • Links are indicated by numbers enclosed in square brackets e.g. [3].
  • To select a link, enter the number after the prompt at the bottom of the screen.
  • Basic help in program, extensive help available in hypertext.
  • Commands.
  • Command Line Syntax.
