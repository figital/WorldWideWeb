Conclusion

Write hypertext linking to other documents. Now, you get
  • Online help on all systems
  • Editing in markup, (or wysiwyg on NeXT)
  • Network access
  • Browsing with DynaText (?)
  • Generation of ASCII text (PS, RTF on NeXT)
Later, you get
  • Full text indexing for initial query
  • Pretty document generation
  • Fancy browsers on more platforms (X)
  • Links to more and more data on the net
