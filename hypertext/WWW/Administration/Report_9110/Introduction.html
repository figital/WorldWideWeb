Introduction

The "World-Wide Web" (W3) project provides the software for a distributed information system. Incorporating the concepts of information retrieval and hypertext into one system, W3 enhances group work and information.

W3 is a state-of-the art project targetted at specific needs of CERN groups, including experiments, accelerator and central support groups. It has also attracted interest from many groups outside CERN.
