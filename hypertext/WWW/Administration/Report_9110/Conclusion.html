Conclusion

The initial phase of the WWW project demonstrated that it could go in many ways and has a very exciting future. We need to carefully plan our use of time in order to achieve what is most important for CERN and HEP.

We need to strengthen the team, investigate moving it into a single location, and also by increasing the manpower within CERN by one or two interested programmers and one librarian.

Tim Berners-Lee, Robert Cailliau, Jean-Francois Groff, Bernd Pollermann, September 1991.
