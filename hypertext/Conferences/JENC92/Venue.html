Venue

JENC3 will take place in Innsbruck, Austria. The city of Innsbruck is situated in the middle of Tirol, one of the most famous holiday areas in Austria. Innsbruck offers history and traditions as well as an up-to-date infrastructure.

The conference will be organized in the Innsbruck Convention Centre - Kongresshaus Innsbruck - a spacious centre of high international standard. The conference aims at an attendance of no more than 500 participants registered on a first come - first served basis.
