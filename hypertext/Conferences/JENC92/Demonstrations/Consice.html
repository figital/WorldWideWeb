CONCISE demonstration

The demonstration will show the CONCISE interactive service. This will be a live demonstration accessing the CONCISE server over IXI. The demonstration will show:
  • How CONCISE may be accessed using a simple terminal attatched to the IXI or a public X.25 network;
  • How the information in CONCISE is structured;
  • How a user can browse the information;
  • How a user can search for information;
  • How Special Interest Groups are using CONCISE;
The demonstration will be located on the COSINE stand and will be run throughout the conference.
