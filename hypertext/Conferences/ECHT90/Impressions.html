Impressions of RC

Here is a list of impressions that I came away with:
  • Throughout tutorial 2 and also the papers presented on the following days, it became clear that serious hypertext is done starting from structured markup of documents using SGML. Today's diversity is the necessary result of people trying out different ways of doing things.
  • We should form a working group on hypertexts for heterogeneous platform environments (such as HEP)
