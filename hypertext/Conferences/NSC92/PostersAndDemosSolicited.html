Posters and Demonstrations

A poster wall will be available to participants for the display of their posters and projects. A terminal room with connectivity to EARN and the Internet will be available to delegates.

A room will be available for workstations and PCs to be used for demonstrations. An Ethernet connected to the Internet will be available in the room. Connectivity to the Internet will be via a 64Kbps line to CNUCE. The minimum bandwidth between CNUCE and CERN is 512Kbps. People interested in setting up demonstrations may send their questions to NSCINFO@FRORS12.BITNET
