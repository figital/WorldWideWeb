RARE WORKING GROUP 3

1. INTRODUCTION

RARE (Reseaux Associes pour la Recherche Europeenne) is the association of users and providers of European research oriented networks. RARE Working Group 3 (WG3) was set up in order to coordinate and harmonise the collection and provision of information of use to the member bodies of RARE.WG3 comprises two subgroups:
  • The User Support and Information Services subgroup (USIS);
  • The Directory Services subgroup (DS).
Members of the group are national representatives, typically one per subgroup nominated by the national RARE contacts, and invited experts including representatives from European-wide research networks. All members contribute to the work of WG3 on a voluntary basis.

The work of the group is closely related to that of the other RARE Working Groups.

The Working Group meets 3 to 4 times a year to discuss actual developments and future work items related to the activities mentioned in its terms of reference. Outside these meetings discussions and document preparation and revision takes place by E-mail through distribution lists.

3. COSINE SUB-PROJECTS

The following COSINE Sub-Projects were proposed by RARE WG3 and are currently being monitored by the relevant subgroup.
  • COSINE sub-project P2.2 (Support and Information Services). This project will provide pilot European User Support and Information Services. The contract is held by Level 7 (UK). Start date Jan'91. See user guide.
  • COSINE sub-project P3 (Pilot activities to support typical International User Groups). The contract for the first phase of the project was awarded to Logica. Start date July '91.
  • COSINE sub-project P2.1 (Pilot International Directory Services). The contract for this project is held by University College London.
