news.future

news.future is for the discussion of near and long term future expansions of Usenet or of other similar news networks.

Some topics that would be appropriate for the group are

  • advanced propogation methods (including broadcast)
  • advanced storage and retreival of news
  • adding hypertext methods/technology to news
  • advanced namespace and distribution concepts
  • article selection and rating systems
  • other topics relevant to the development and design of advanced news networks
news.future will be unmoderated.

Discussions of other existing electronic news services and of future news services would be encouraged, that the group can be as creative as possible in discussing and analyzing avenues for expansion of news networks.
