The "Incomplete Guide" to the Internet

From: cfarmer@ncsa.uiuc.EDU (Chuck Farmer) Subject: Incomplete Guide to the Internet
October 1992
The "Incomplete Guide ..." is now available for anonymous FTP at: ftp.ncsa.uiuc.edu in the /misc directory.

This manual basically a how to book for teachers and students. The first half of the text is devoted to the mechanics of telecommunications, how to connect, what to do once you are connected, etc. The second have the manual is devoted to current telecommunications projects, past successful projects, and resources. The resources include FTP sites, open BBS's and networks, Free-Nets, subscription services, and where to get more information on each resource.

This resource was complied by the Living Lab program (NSF funded) at NCSA as an attempt to encourage the proliferation of HPCC use in the K-12 classroom. We welcome your comments and suggestions.
