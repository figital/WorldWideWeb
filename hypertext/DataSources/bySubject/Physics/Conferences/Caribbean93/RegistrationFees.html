Registration Fees

We want to be able to organize this school without having to ask for any kind of registration fees. However, several funding institutions will not give their definitive answer before the end of 1992. This makes impossible any evaluation of the amount of such fees. Should we receive a positive answer from funding institutions other than those mentionned in the next section, the registration fees will be decreased or even totally suppressed. In the worst case, the cost will not be higher than 1000 FF per person and this will be collected during the conference.
